A Glimpse at My Artwork

My name is Mariam Ibrahim and I am a Pictorial Art major at SJSU. I have always gravitated toward art. Anything that is full of color, lack thereof, art created on a piece of paper, art on canvas, on boards, wood-panelsâ€” and in my case when I was younger, a white board with expo markers. I never had a set goal or idea of what I really wanted to create, I would just pick up a pencil and start somewhere. Often these little drawings that I did, would come out to be random faces, trees with the sun in the corner and V shaped birds emerging from bubble shaped clouds. As I grow, and learn more about art, i realize that it is more than just lines and colors on a surface- to me, it is about being able to explore and learn more about myself. Hi, and thank you for joining me on this journey of trying to explore art.
